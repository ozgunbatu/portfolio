üìä Fleet Utilization = Profitability Driver: 85% utilization (15% gap between subscriptions) = breakeven. 90%+ = strong margins. FINN optimizes via dynamic pricing (lower rates during gaps), shorter subscription lengths (more cycles/year).
üí∞ Residual Value Risk = Biggest Threat: COVID used car spike (2021) helped margins. 2023 crash hurt. Solution: OEM partnerships (BMW, VW) with buyback guarantees at predetermined residuals.

Strategic Recommendations

1. Double Down on B2B Corporate

Rationale: B2B customers (SMEs needing company cars) have 2x LTV, lower churn, predictable contracts.

  • Target: 500-5,000 employee companies (sales teams, field service)
  • Offer fleet management dashboard, bulk pricing

Impact: ‚Ç¨200M ARR from B2B, 60% gross margins vs 40% B2C

2. OEM Partnerships for Residual Guarantees

Rationale: Hedge depreciation risk. OEMs want distribution for EVs, FINN wants price certainty.

  • Negotiate: BMW/VW guarantee 70% residual after 24mo
  • In exchange: FINN commits to 5,000 EV orders/year

Impact: Eliminates downside risk, unlocks debt financing

3. Marketplace Model (Asset-Light Transition)

Rationale: Own fleet = capital-intensive. Launch marketplace: dealerships list cars, FINN takes 15% commission.

  • Transition from ‚Ç¨375M fleet to ‚Ç¨0 (dealerships own cars)
  • FINN = platform (insurance, contracts, payments)

Impact: Asset-light = higher valuation multiples, faster scale
