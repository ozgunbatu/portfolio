Asset-Light Model
Business Model: FlixBus operates an asset-light platform connecting 300+ bus operators (supply) with 75M+ passengers (demand). Partners own buses and drivers, FlixBus handles branding, routing, pricing, and ticketing‚Äîtaking 30-35% platform fee while operators handle operations.

Strategic Challenge

Can a technology platform compete with state-owned railways (Deutsche Bahn) and private car ownership in Germany's regulated, environmentally-conscious mobility market‚Äîand do so profitably without owning physical assets?
