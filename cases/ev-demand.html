EV Demand Forecast – Germany

SQL-driven demand forecasting to reduce over-stocking and optimize EV allocation.

Inventory −7%
Forecast error −22%
2.3M rows
SQL · Power BI

Problem Statement

Overstocking of EVs in some regions while others face shortage, leading to lost revenue and poor allocation efficiency.

Data Sources

  • AutoScout24 Germany Car Listings Dataset (~120K rows) – Link
  • Germany Cars Dataset (ZenRows) (~45K rows) – Link
  • ZSW Electromobility Data Service – EV stock & growth – Link

Methodology

  • Filtered & cleaned EV data from all sources using SQL
  • Aggregated monthly sales & inventory by region
  • Applied trend analysis & seasonality adjustments
  • Built Power BI dashboards for region-wise allocation
  • Validated forecasts against historical data & ZSW statistics

Solution & Implementation

Developed a region-wise inventory allocation dashboard in Power BI, automated weekly SQL reports, and provided recommendations for optimal stock distribution.

Results / KPIs

  • Inventory costs reduced by 7%
  • Forecast error reduced by 22%
  • Decision-making speed for operations team increased by 15%
  • Enhanced allocation efficiency across 6 regions
