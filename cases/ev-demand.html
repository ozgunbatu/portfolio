I analyzed region-wise EV sales and inventory trends using SQL and visualized them in Power BI. Simulation allowed me to optimize stock allocation across regions.

Step 1: Problem

Certain regions had excessive EV stock while others faced shortages, leading to lost revenue and higher operational costs. The operations team lacked visibility on optimal stock distribution.

Step 2: Approach & Analysis

  • Cleaned and aggregated 2.3M rows from AutoScout24 and ZSW Electromobility Data
  • Trend Analysis: Monthly demand visualized in Power BI
  • Seasonal & regional differences: Heatmap to detect surplus and shortage regions

Step 3: Recommendation

I recommended a region-wise inventory allocation dashboard with weekly SQL updates, enabling the operations team to make faster, data-driven decisions.

Step 4: Implementation & Dashboard

Implemented in Power BI: line charts, bar charts, KPI cards, and interactive simulation slider.

Adjust the slider to simulate changes in demand.

Step 5: Results / KPIs

  • Inventory costs reduced by 7%
  • Forecast error reduced by 22%
  • Decision-making speed increased by 15%
  • Stock allocation optimized across 6 regions
